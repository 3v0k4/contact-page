Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled by Basal.

Basal Does NOT Collect Any Personal Information

Basal does not collect, use, save, or have access to any of the data recorded in the app. Everything is local to your device and never sent anywhere.

App Analytics

To help better understand how people use Basal and to continue improving it, we use analytics information provided by the App Store (as any other application on the store). These analytics provide statistics that are completely anonymous and cannot personally identify you in any way.
